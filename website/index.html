About

Problem Statement

In society today, many people feel the need to not only write down their schedules, but also input them on an electronic medium.

To solve this issue we propose Calvin Calendar. Calvin creates a more seamless interaction between tactile and electronic schedulers. It is a tangible interface that provides a link between handwritten planners and their digital counterparts. Our soluction consists of a concrete idea that includes writing your schedule within a tangible notebook with a Livescribe pen. Once an event is written into a users notebook it will update to Calvin where your events will post into a library and can be added to select days.
